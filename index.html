What We Do

Plumbing Services We Offer

From emergency repairs to full installations, our certified plumbers handle all types of residential and commercial plumbing jobs across Johannesburg and Pretoria.

Burst Pipe Repairs

Fast, professional repair of burst or damaged water supply pipes — minimising disruption and water damage to your property.

Leak Detection

Non-invasive leak detection technology to locate hidden leaks in walls, slabs and underground pipes without unnecessary damage.

Blocked Drain Cleaning

High-pressure jetting and professional drain clearing for kitchen, bathroom and stormwater drains — fully cleared and flowing again.

Geyser Repair & Installation

Same-day geyser repairs and installations. We supply and fit all major brands — including solar and heat pump geysers.

Toilet & Bathroom Repairs

Toilet cistern repairs, running toilets, blocked toilets and full bathroom fixture replacements and reseals done right.

Bathroom Renovations

Planning a bathroom upgrade? Our team handles all plumbing for full bathroom renovations — from design to completion.

Tap & Faucet Repairs

Dripping taps and leaking faucets waste thousands of litres of water. We replace washers, cartridges and fixtures quickly.

Gas Geyser Installation

Certified gas geyser installations for load-shedding-proof hot water. We fit Rinnai, Bosch, and other leading brands.

Shower Installation

From mixer showers to full enclosures — our team ensures proper pipe sizing, pressure balance and watertight sealing.
